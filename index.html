Le Fouquet

Le Fouquet Boutique Hotel
A time-honored experience with a modern twist

The interior of Le Fouquet hotel is defined by letting letting natural beauty shine. The focus is to create a relaxing atmosphere by highlighting the use of walnut wood, ash-tree wood as well as iron elements.Inspired by the boutique hotels of Paris, but without straying too far from the identity of Korça, the aesthetics of Le Fouquet stands out for its warm color palettes that create a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.

Another special detail is the volume of glass that starts in the restaurant and ends at the reception, which has not only an aesthetic purpose, but also ensures good natural lighting in the restaurant.

The Restaurant

At Le Foutique Hotel you can enjoy a variety of dishes, starting from the traditional ones, such as lakori, traditional meatballs, fried pancakes, petanik, etc. up to the gourmet recipes. The basis of the menu is the traditional one, but brought with a modern spirit to offer you an unforgettable culinary experience.

Experience

The City

Le Fouquet is located in the heart of the city of Korça, the unofficial cultural capital of Albania known as the City of Serenades, the City of Love or Little Paris. A few meters from the hotel, you can enjoy the picturesque views of thw city, the cobbled streets, its significant architecture, beautiful churches, mosques, museums and of course the fresh air.
